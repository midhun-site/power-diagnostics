Our Product Range

From circuit protection to control systems, we stock quality electrical components to keep your operations running smoothly and safely.

High Voltage AC / DC Testers & Test Systems
High Voltage AC / DC Testers & Test Systems

Our range of high voltage AC/DC testers and test systems are designed for comprehensive testing of electrical equipment and systems. These testers provide accurate measurements and diagnostics for insulation resistance, dielectric strength, and other critical parameters. They are essential tools for ensuring the safety and reliability of electrical installations in various industries, including power generation, manufacturing, and maintenance.

AC & DC Clamp Meters
AC & DC Clamp Meters

AC & DC Clamp Meters are essential tools for measuring current without direct contact with the conductor. They provide accurate readings for both alternating and direct currents, making them versatile for various electrical applications. These meters are ideal for troubleshooting, maintenance, and installation tasks in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

Multimeters
Multimeters

Digital multimeters are versatile instruments used for measuring voltage, current, and resistance in electrical circuits. They provide accurate readings and are essential tools for troubleshooting, maintenance, and installation tasks in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

High Voltage Insulation Testers & Megohmmeters
High Voltage Insulation Testers & Megohmmeters

High voltage insulation testers and megohmmeters are essential tools for measuring insulation resistance and ensuring the safety and reliability of electrical systems. These devices help detect insulation deterioration, prevent electrical failures, and maintain compliance with industry standards in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

Production Line Safety Testers
Production Line Safety Testers

Production line safety testers are designed to ensure the safety and compliance of electrical products during manufacturing. These testers perform a variety of safety checks, including insulation resistance, ground continuity, and leakage current tests, helping manufacturers maintain high safety standards and meet regulatory requirements.

Relay Test Sets & Circuit Breaker Analysers
Relay Test Sets & Circuit Breaker Analysers

Relay test sets and circuit breaker analysers are essential tools for testing and maintaining protective relays and circuit breakers. These instruments help ensure the reliability and safety of electrical power systems by accurately measuring relay performance, timing, and trip characteristics, as well as diagnosing potential issues in circuit breakers.

Secondary Current Injection Test Sets
Secondary Current Injection Test Sets

Secondary current injection test sets are specialized instruments used for testing the performance and accuracy of protective relays and circuit breakers. These test sets simulate fault conditions by injecting controlled currents into the secondary circuits of relays, allowing technicians to verify their response and ensure the reliability of electrical power systems.

Primary Current Injection Test Sets
Primary Current Injection Test Sets

Primary current injection test sets are used for testing the performance and accuracy of high-current electrical equipment, such as circuit breakers and transformers. These test sets provide precise current injection to simulate real-world operating conditions, ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical power systems.
