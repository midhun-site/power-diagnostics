Repair Service

Power Diagnostics specialized in the repair, maintenance, and calibration of high-voltage and electrical test equipment, ensuring optimal performance, accuracy, and safety. Our team of experienced engineers is equipped to handle a wide range of diagnostic and testing instruments, delivering reliable solutions that meet industry standards and compliance requirements.

With a commitment to precision and quality, we provide comprehensive services including fault diagnosis, component-level repair, preventive maintenance, and certification support. Our goal is to minimize downtime and extend the operational life of your critical testing equipment.

Step 1: Initial Assessment

Before we undertake any investigation or repair we will inform you to gain approval to proceed.

Step 2: Quote & Approval

If you wish to proceed you will be sent a full repair service quote of the work required. No work will be carried out prior to your acceptance and a purchase order to cover the work.

Step 3: Timely Delivery

Power Diagnostics strives to return your repaired instrument to you by the committed date following the receipt of your purchase order.
